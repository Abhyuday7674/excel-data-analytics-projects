## Project Context
TechnoMart is an Indian electronics retail chain operating 15 stores and selling
smartphones, laptops, tablets, and accessories. The company has collected
12 months of sales data, including units sold, prices, total sales, and
customer counts.

Management wants to use Excel-based statistical techniques to better understand
sales performance, variability, and business patterns across stores and products.

## Business Objectives
This analysis aims to:
- Understand overall sales performance
- Detect variability and sales patterns
- Support inventory and marketing decisions
- Interpret statistical results for business use

## Analysis Performed
The Excel analysis covers:
- Descriptive statistics (mean, median, mode, variance, standard deviation)
- Sales distribution and variability analysis
- Z-score and probability-based interpretation
- Pivot table analysis by store, product, and time
- Business risk assessment and recommendations

## Tools & Techniques Used
- Microsoft Excel
- Statistical functions
- Pivot Tables
- Data aggregation and interpretation
- Business-focused analytical reasoning

## Key Business Takeaways
- Sales show significant variability across stores and months
- High-performing products and locations drive a large share of revenue
- Demand volatility impacts inventory planning and cash flow
- Statistical insights can support more informed marketing and stocking decisions
